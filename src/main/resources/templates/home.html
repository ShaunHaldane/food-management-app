Welcome to Food Management App

This app helps you track the amount of money that you are spending and wasting on food

You simply add your shopping items using the add item button and let the app warn you about how long each item has till expiration date.

If you dont eat the food item, click the waste button and it will be added to a backlog of wasted food items.

You will be able to monitor how much money you are wasting (Euros) on discarded food throughout the year.

Just create an account and login to get started.
